All following items are crafted on lead forge unless stated otherwise

#Lead-mixed pickaxe 
Pickaxe using the best atributes of materials its made of.
Has 500 durability,quicker mining time and abillity to break bunker shielding
Recipe:1 thorium pickaxe,6 adamantium-lead bars,18 cobalt-lead bars

#Lead-infused adamantium sword
Structural elements of the sword are swapped for lead alloy,significantly increasing durability
Has 300 durability and slightly beter attack damage
Recipe:1 adamantium sword,10 adamantium-lead bars
